About Us

Spencer Guitars was established way back earlier this week when founder Danny Spencer needed something to make his website about in his CS 260 class. Though we don't yet have a physical location, or any actual guitars, we put our customer satisfaction at the top of our priorities.

For reals though, if you gave me enough money I probably would go buy you a guitar.

Can I also just say, I really enjoyed doing this project because I love guitars and I love coding. And I finally figured out how to use CSS grid, so that's a plus.
